What is Creathon?

A 5-hour college-level innovation event designed to cultivate practical implementation skills.

Innovation Meets Execution

Creathon challenges students to build real-world solutions under pressure. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and industry reality using cutting-edge AI, Machine Learning, and Full Stack technologies. It's not just about ideas; it's about building working prototypes.

Our Goal

Bridge classroom learning with real-time development experience through hands-on problem solving.

Focus Areas

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Full Stack Development — technologies shaping tomorrow.

Team Spirit

Work in teams of 3–4 members to collaborate, ideate, and build complete working solutions.

The Playbook

Competition Rules

📋 General Guidelines

  • 01

    Batch Division

    Teams will be divided into batches based on the total number of registrations. For example, 100 teams will be split into 4 batches of 25 teams each.

  • 02

    Problem Assignment

    Each batch will be assigned unique themes and problem statements to solve, ensuring a diverse range of solutions.

  • 03

    Qualifying Criteria

    The top team from each batch will secure their spot and proceed directly to the Grand Finale (Round 2).

🏁 The Rounds

Phase 1

Round 1: Batch Heats

Teams tackle assigned problems within their batches. High intensity, fast-paced solution building focused on core themes.

Finals

Round 2: Grand Finale

The ultimate challenge. Finalist teams face a complex problem statement. The best execution and solution will be crowned the Creathon Winner.
